# Northwind Traders Sales Analytics & Visualization

## Project Overview
This Business Analytics project analyzes Northwind Traders sales performance from 2021–2023 to identify customer behavior patterns, seasonal trends, product performance, and regional sales opportunities.

## Business Problem
Northwind Traders wanted to understand revenue drivers, customer retention, acquisition decline, seasonal demand, product mix, and regional sales behavior.

## Tools Used
- Excel / Tableau / Power BI
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics
- Customer Segmentation
- Sales Trend Analysis

## Key Analyses
1. Repeat vs. New Customer Analysis
2. Average Order Value vs. Purchase Frequency
3. Product Mix Analysis
4. Seasonal Sales Trend Analysis
5. Regional Sales Performance

## Key Insights
- Repeat customers generated the majority of revenue.
- New customer acquisition declined sharply.
- High-AOV customers were under-engaged.
- Beverages and Dairy dominated sales.
- Sales peaked from May to August.
- Regional product preferences varied significantly.

## Recommendations
- Improve customer acquisition campaigns.
- Build loyalty and bundling campaigns for high-AOV customers.
- Use Activity-Based Costing to understand profitability.
- Localize marketing and inventory by region.
- Plan inventory and promotions around seasonal peaks.
